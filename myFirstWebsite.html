White Collar

White Collar is a USA Network TV series created by Jeff Eastin, starring Tim DeKay as FBI Agent Peter Burke and Matt Bomer as criminal Neal Caffrey. The show is about a white collar criminal, Neal Caffrey, who has been running from the FBI for years. He finally gets caught, receives a 4 year sentence in jail, and in his last 3 months of his sentence, he escapes for a girl. FBI Agent Peter Burke ends up catching him again, but instead of putting him back in jail, he hires him as a criminal informant for the FBI. Caffrey and Burke set out to catch different sought after criminals, and the FBI soon realizes that Caffrey's insights and instincts are a rare commodity.

Character Descriptions

Neal George Caffrey is charming, sophisticated, and is considered to be one of the world's greatest con artists. Neal was an elusive White Collar criminal, and after a three-year chase, he was eventually apprehended by FBI agent Peter Burke.
